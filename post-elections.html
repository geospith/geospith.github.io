Local Elections 2023
Kifisia | Nea Etyrhraia | Ekali


In October 2023, I ran in the local elections for councelor in Kifisia, Nea Etyrhraia and Ekali (press, press, press, press, press). This was my first time I stood for elections as a political candidate.

The core aim of my political agenda was to improve the quality of services of the council and the everyday experience of the citizens. I proposed using modern tools and technologies (e.g. artificial intelligence) to bring about the digtial transformation of the council, streamline its commumnication with the citizens, and improve the productivity and efficiency of its everyday operations. Moreover, I expressed a strong interest in increasing the opportunities for engagement of young adults in decision making, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

In spite of receiving no funding for my marketing my campaing, my content went viral and reached more than half a million Greeks (i.e. at least 5% of the total population) (press, press, press).
